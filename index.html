The Art Institute of Chicago

Chicago, Illinois

This is an oil on canvas painting by Edward Hopper titled Nighthawks. I had the joy of seeing this painting in person at The Art Institute of Chicago this past Summer of 2022. Despite living so close to Chicago, this was my first visit! It was a particularly hot Summer day, but incredible collection of artwork at the institute made up for it.

Millennium Trail

Lindenhurst, Illinois

This trail lies in the back of my neighborhood. I have spent hours walking my dogs here, biking with my mom, catching up with friends, and going for runs whenever nice weather came around.

My Home in Illinois

Lindenhurst, Illinois

An hour north of Chicago and 15 minutes south of the Wisconsin border lies Lindenhurst, Illinois. This is where I grew up in my family of six. I often spent my time outside whenever the weather would permit or my family and I would head to downtown Chicago to visit my older brother. While Lindenhurst may be a small town, below lies a collection of photos representative of my time living in Illinois.

The Art Institute of Chicago

Chicago, Illinois

This is a painting by Edward Hopper titled Nighthawks. I had the joy of seeing this painting in person at The Art Institute of Chicago this past Summer of 2022. Despite living so close to Chicago, this was my first visit to the Art Institute! It was a particularly hot Summer day, but the incredible collection of artwork I sw this day made up for it. I feel fortunate to have grown up so close to a major city filled with memorable landmarks.

Millennium Trail

Lindenhurst, Illinois

This trail lies in the back of my neighborhood. I have spent hours walking my dogs here, biking with my mom, catching up with friends, and going for runs whenever nice weather came around. We didn't have this trail until I was in middle school, and I am lucky that it came when it did!

Millennium Trail

Lindenhurst, Illinois

Here is another picture of the Millennium trail at the same boardwalk, but this time captured at sunset. Sunset walks have always been a way for me to relax at the end of day with some good music or an interesting podcast.

The Loop

Chicago, Illinois

This is a picture of the Chicago Loop taken from a boat on the Chicago River. My family and I rented a boat to take out on the river and spent a lovely day cruising through the river.

Millennium Trail

Linenhurst, Illinois

Again, the Millennium Trail! This picture is taken at the entrance of the trail. Lindenhurst is a small town.

N Hunt Club Rd

Old Mill Creek, Illinois

I drove down this road every day during one Summer to get to work. At the beginning of this road is a horse stable with white fences and vast fields. I loved seeing the horses grazing every morning with the sun shining bright in the early mornings on my drives to work.

Third Lake

Third Lake, Illinois

This sunset is pictured over Third Lake. Over the Summer, we would take my friends boat out onto this lake and the sunsets never failed to amaze me.

Wrigley Field

Chicago, Illinois

I took this photo after a Cubs win! Don't ask me who was playing, though.

Wrigley Field Entrance

Chicago, Illinois

Wrigley Field is also a great concert venue. I saw Dead & Company for the first time in Summer 2022 with my Dad. It was an awesome concert and Wrigleyville was packed!

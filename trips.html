Chappaquiddick Island

2019

A Sunset to Remember

Chappaquiddick Island is located on the eastern end of Martha's Vineyard. My Aunt owns a house on Chappy, and the sunsets never fail to amaze me.

A Fallen Chimney

This old chimney sits on the shoreline that backs up to my Aunt's house. It is a landmark of my childhood trips!

Gainesville, Florida

Alligator Galore

While visiting visiting the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida with a friend of mine, we spontaneously visited La Chua trail after being told we could see some wild alligators here. If you look closesly, alligators can be seen lining the far shore of the Alachua Sink pictured here and one lurks in the water, as well.
